Finally, after getting all of the needed parts for the pocketwatch, you are able to return to your time.

Upon traveling back to your time, you are right back to where you where when you first picked up the watch.

You look back at your hand to find the pocketwatch has been replaced by a piece of paper.

There's something written on the paper. It says:

"I had to take this back. I can't trust anybody else with time travel. Thanks for fixing it for me though. -AW"

Well, it's time to head home anyways. You were tired beforehand, but now you are even feel like you've been awake for several more hours. Checking the time shows that not even a minute has passed.

Time Travel is a strange thing isn't it?
