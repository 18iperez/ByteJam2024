Traveling to the 1920s, You find yourself in a bustling city.

You notice a jewelry store down the street. Maybe there will be a part there.

Looking inside, there seems to be a part on display. The shopkeeper says that he found it recently and was thinking of using it in a necklace.

You offer $25 for the part by itself. He is suprised that you have that much money on hand and are willing to give it away.

The shopkeeper gives you the part, and says that since you seem to be a weathly client, he offers a position in "the family business".

You decline his offer and leave before anything happens.
